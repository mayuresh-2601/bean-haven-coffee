Our Story

Bean Haven Coffee is more than just a coffee shop — it is a place where people gather, relax, and enjoy great coffee.

Bean Haven Coffee was founded in 2018 by coffee enthusiast Mayuresh Kasar. His dream was to create a welcoming café where people could enjoy premium coffee, relax with friends, and experience the true art of coffee brewing. Today, Bean Haven Coffee serves hundreds of happy customers and continues to grow as a favorite destination for coffee lovers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve high-quality coffee made from ethically sourced beans while creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere for every customer.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a community hub where coffee lovers, students, professionals, and friends can gather and enjoy a great café experience.

Coffee Bean Sourcing

We carefully source our coffee beans from the best coffee-growing regions in the world.

  • Brazil — Smooth and chocolatey flavor
  • Colombia — Balanced and aromatic beans
  • Ethiopia — Fruity and floral coffee notes
  • India — Rich and bold coffee taste
